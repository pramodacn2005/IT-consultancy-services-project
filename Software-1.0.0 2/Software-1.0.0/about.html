About Company

Founded in 2022 by Vishal, Pramoda, and Bharat, Trio Bits embarked on its journey as a humble software consultancy. With a shared passion for technology and innovation, the trio quickly realized the potential of the burgeoning mobile app market. In 2023, they released their first mobile app, which garnered positive feedback and laid the foundation for their future success. As smartphones gained traction globally, Trio Bits capitalized on this trend, focusing its efforts on mobile app development. By 2023, the company had established itself as a prominent player in the industry, with a portfolio of successful apps spanning various categories. In 2024, recognizing the shifting landscape of technology, Trio Bits expanded its services to include web development, catering to the growing demand for online platforms. This strategic move not only diversified their offerings but also positioned them as a comprehensive solution provider in the digital sphere. Additionally, Trio Bits ventured into the realm of productivity software, leveraging its expertise to develop innovative tools aimed at enhancing efficiency and streamlining workflows for businesses and individuals alike. Throughout its evolution, Trio Bits has remained committed to its core values of quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction. With a talented team driving its continued growth and a dedication to staying at the forefront of technological advancements, the company looks poised for a promising future in the ever-evolving tech industry.
